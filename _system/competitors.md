## Key Differentiators to Emphasise

- **Direct manufacturer** — not a marketplace, not a broker. One facility, one team.
- **Full prototyping toolkit** — CNC + 3D printing + sheet metal + injection molding + vacuum casting + wire EDM. Competitors like Protolabs are CNC-heavy.
- **DFM review included** — flagged before quoting, not after production starts.
- **Dongguan pricing** — cost-competitive without sacrificing tolerance or documentation.
- **Documentation standard** — dimensional inspection report + material cert + CoC on every order, not on request.

## Rules for Alternatives Pages

- Never fabricate competitor specs — only cite what is publicly stated on their website
- Frame differences as buyer-observable outcomes, not vague claims
- One FAQ per alternatives page must compare TOP PROTO to the named competitor spec-to-spec
- Never use: "better", "superior", "best" — use specific numbers and outcomes instead
