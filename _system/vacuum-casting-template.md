## Section 8: The Process
**Design:** RS-02 variant — 5-step horizontal timeline. Light blue background.
**Heading:** How Vacuum Casting Works

1. **Master Pattern** — CNC machined or SLA printed to exact geometry
2. **Silicone Mould** — Poured around master in vacuum chamber, cured, then split
3. **Casting** — Polyurethane resin mixed, poured, and cured under vacuum
4. **Demoulding** — Part removed, sprues trimmed, any inserts checked
5. **Finishing** — Surface treatment, painting, or assembly as specified
