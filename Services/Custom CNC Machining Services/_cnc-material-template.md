## Section 8: Design Guidelines for [Material]

**Design:** Table format. White background.
**Heading:** [Material] CNC Design Guidelines

| Feature | Recommendation | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| Min wall thickness | [spec — e.g. 0.5mm for Al, 0.8mm for SS] | Thinner walls vibrate during cutting — dimension loss and chatter |
| Min hole diameter | 1mm (up to 10:1 depth ratio) | Deeper holes require peck drilling and may reduce tolerance |
| Thread depth | 1.5× diameter minimum | Ensures full thread engagement and pull-out strength |
| Internal corner radius | 0.5mm minimum | End mills have finite diameter — sharp internal corners are not achievable |
| Undercuts | Possible with lollipop cutters — flag in design | Non-standard setups add cost; design for accessibility where possible |
| Tolerances on mating faces | State tolerance explicitly — don't rely on default | Default ISO 2768-m is ±0.1mm — state ±0.005mm where fit is critical |
| Surface finish callouts | Specify Ra on critical surfaces only | Blanket tight-finish callouts increase cost unnecessarily |
