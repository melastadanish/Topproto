# Precision CNC Machining Services

TOP PROTO manufactures precision CNC machined parts from its facility in Dongguan, China. Standard tolerances of ±0.01 mm (ISO 2768-m) and precision tolerances to ±0.005 mm cover prototypes through low-volume production in metals and engineering plastics. Every quote includes a DFM review and returns within 24 hours.

Services span CNC milling, CNC turning, 5-axis CNC machining, custom CNC machining, and in-house surface finishing — all under ISO 9001:2015 quality management.

---

## CNC Services

### [CNC Milling](/cnc-milling-services/)
**Pockets, slots, complex contours — 3-axis and 5-axis**

CNC milling removes material to produce flat surfaces, open pockets, profiled contours, and complex 3D forms. TOP PROTO runs 3-axis and 5-axis milling in aluminium, steel, titanium, and engineering plastics. Maximum milling envelope: 4000 × 1500 × 600 mm.

---

### [CNC Turning](/cnc-turning-services/)
**Cylindrical parts, shafts, bushings — live tooling and Swiss turning**

CNC turning produces rotational parts — shafts, bushings, threaded components, and tube fittings. Live tooling capability adds milled features in a single setup. Swiss turning handles slender, high-precision small-diameter parts.

---

### [5-Axis CNC Machining](/5-axis-cnc-machining-services/)
**Complex geometry, compound angles — single setup**

5-axis simultaneous machining reaches five faces of a part without repositioning. This reduces setup error, cuts lead time on complex geometry, and holds tighter tolerances on compound-angle features than sequential 3-axis setups.

---

### [Custom CNC Machining](/custom-cnc-machining-services/)
**Any geometry, any material — full-service CNC**

Custom CNC machining covers parts that fall outside standard process categories: multi-feature housings, hybrid geometries, close-tolerance assemblies, and one-off prototypes requiring process decisions early in quoting. Upload your drawing and TOP PROTO selects the process path.

---

### [Surface Finishing](/surface-finishing-services/)
**Anodizing, passivation, powder coating, and more — in-house**

Surface finishing changes corrosion resistance, hardness, appearance, and dimensional tolerances. Available finishes include Type II and Type III anodizing, powder coating, passivation, electroplating, nickel plating, bead blasting, black oxide, electropolishing, brushed finish, and polishing.

## How It Works

**Step 1 — Upload your STEP file and 2D drawing**
Submit geometry through the quote portal. A 2D drawing with GD&T callouts, tolerances, and surface finish requirements helps the engineering team review your part before quoting.

**Step 2 — Receive a DFM-reviewed quote within 24 hours**
An engineer reviews your file for manufacturability — wall thickness, hole depth-to-diameter ratios, tolerance feasibility, and material selection. The quote includes any DFM flags with recommended fixes. No DFM charge.

**Step 3 — Approve and enter production**
After approval, machining starts. In-process inspection checks critical dimensions at each setup change. Parts machined to your drawing callouts, not to default tolerances.

**Step 4 — CMM inspection and dimensional report**
First articles and tolerance-critical orders receive coordinate measuring machine (CMM) inspection. Dimensional reports are available on request.

**Step 5 — Ships with documentation**
Parts ship with a certificate of conformance. Material certifications and full dimensional reports available on request. Carriers: DHL, FedEx, UPS, sea freight.

## Quality System

TOP PROTO operates under ISO 9001:2015 quality management. The quality process runs across three stages.

**Pre-production:** Technical assessment of your file, material verification against your specification, and DFM review before machining starts.

**In-production:** On-site inspection at each setup change. Critical dimensions checked against drawing callouts during machining, not only at final inspection.

**Final inspection:** Full-dimensional check before parts leave the facility. CMM inspection available on request for tight-tolerance or first-article orders. Dimensional reports, material certifications, and certificates of conformance available on request.

All standard orders are produced in-house. TOP PROTO does not subcontract standard CNC machining orders to third-party shops.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is precision CNC machining and what tolerances can it hold?

Precision CNC machining uses computer-controlled cutting tools to remove material from a solid block, producing parts to a programmed geometry. Standard CNC machining at TOP PROTO holds ±0.01 mm per ISO 2768-m. For tight-tolerance features, precision CNC machining reaches ±0.005 mm. Wire EDM — used for slots, cavities, and punch tooling — achieves ±0.002–0.005 mm.

### What CNC processes does TOP PROTO offer?

TOP PROTO offers CNC milling (3-axis and 5-axis), CNC turning with live tooling and Swiss turning capability, 5-axis simultaneous machining, custom CNC machining for complex or multi-feature parts, and wire EDM for precision cavities and profiles. Surface finishing is available in-house on the same order.

### What materials can be precision machined at TOP PROTO?

Metals include aluminium (6061, 7075), stainless steel (303, 304, 316L), titanium (Grade 2, Grade 5), brass, copper, tool steel, and steel alloys. Engineering plastics include ABS, PC, PMMA, POM (Delrin), PA (nylon), PEEK, PP, PTFE, HDPE, and Ultem. Upload your STEP file with material callout — the quote confirms machinability for your specific grade.

### How long does precision CNC machining take?

Standard lead time is 5 business days from order confirmation. Rush orders complete in 1–3 business days subject to capacity. Material-specific lead times: aluminium 3–5 business days; steel and stainless steel 5–7 business days; titanium 7–10 business days. Quotes return within 24 hours including DFM review.

### Is there a minimum order quantity for precision machined parts?

No. TOP PROTO accepts single-part orders. One-off prototypes, small batches, and higher-volume runs follow the same quoting process and quality standards.

### How does TOP PROTO verify dimensional accuracy?

In-process inspection checks critical dimensions at each setup change during machining. CMM inspection — coordinate measuring machine — is available on request for tight-tolerance or first-article orders. Dimensional reports, material certifications, and certificates of conformance are available on request with any order.
