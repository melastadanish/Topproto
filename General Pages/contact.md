## What to Include in Your Message

A complete first message eliminates back-and-forth and accelerates your quote. Include the following:

- **CAD file** — STEP or STL preferred; DXF or DWG for 2D / sheet metal work
- **Technical drawing** — PDF with GD&T callouts, critical tolerances, and thread specifications
- **Material** — specify alloy or grade where relevant (e.g., aluminium 6061-T6, 316L stainless)
- **Surface finish** — anodising, bead blast, powder coat, as-machined, or as-printed
- **Quantity** — prototypes, pilot run, or production volume
- **Target delivery date** — or state if rush lead time applies

Missing any of these items delays your quote while the team follows up. Providing all six fields in your first message typically accelerates your quote toward the 24-hour turnaround.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How quickly will I get a quote?

TOP PROTO returns a DFM-reviewed quote within 24 hours of receiving your files. The review includes feedback on features that may affect machinability, print quality, or cost — so you are not just getting a price, you are getting actionable engineering input. Rush submissions received before midday typically return a quote the same business day.

### What information speeds up my quote?

A complete submission — CAD file, tolerance drawing, material, finish, quantity, and deadline — allows the engineering team to quote without a follow-up email. Incomplete submissions require clarification, which adds time. STEP files with an accompanying PDF drawing produce the most accurate quotes.

### Can I request a quote without a CAD file?

Yes. Send a detailed sketch, PDF drawing, or written description of your part. The team will confirm feasibility and, in most cases, return a budgetary quote. For a firm, production-ready price the team will ask for a CAD file before order confirmation.

### Does TOP PROTO support NDAs and IP protection?

TOP PROTO treats all customer files, drawings, and project details as confidential. State "NDA required" in your first message and the team will send documentation before you share any files. Once the NDA is signed, submit your STEP, STL, DXF, DWG, IGES, or PDF files through the standard quote form and receive a DFM-reviewed price within 24 hours.

### Where is TOP PROTO located and which regions do you ship to?

TOP PROTO manufactures in Dongguan, China. Parts ship worldwide via DHL, FedEx, UPS, and sea freight. TOP PROTO includes standard export documentation with every shipment — include your target delivery date in your quote request so the team can recommend the appropriate shipping method.
